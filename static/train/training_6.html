Training

In the study you will be shown one of the five kinds of graphics you have just seen for a short amount of time.

Immediately afterwards a black and white grid will appear on your screen to “mask” the graphic you have just seen.

You will then be asked two questions.

  1. You will be asked to identify the type of graphic.

  2. You will also be asked to describe the number of elements like lines, clusters, bars, sections/slices in a graph.
  • sections or slices in a Pie Chart.
  • clusters in a Scatter Plot.
  • bars in a Bar Chart.
  • clusters in a Node Link Diagram.
  • lines in a Line Graph.

    • You are encouraged to answer all the questions as quickly and accurately as possible. If you are not sure, try to make the best guess.

    At the end of the study, if you agree to participate the prize draw, we offer a gift card of a randomly selected participant with good performance.
